About me

Computer Engineering student at Federico II and Apple Developer Academy student. I am a lifelong learner and highly adaptable, firmly believing that collaboration and the exchange of ideas are the primary catalysts for innovation. I combine analytical rigor with a user-centric, creative mindset.

I identify myself as an iOS developer with a strong eye for front-end detail and product design. I care deeply about the overlap between engineering and interface, the kind of polish that makes an app feel like it was crafted, not assembled.

I work primarily in Swift and SwiftUI, with experience across many aspects of iOS stack. I also bring a strong design sensibility to my projects, prioritizing UX and accessibility from the very early stages of the development process.
